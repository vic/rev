# Verse-by-Verse Exegesis of the Book of Revelation

**Source texts**: KJV Revelation text; Ranko Stefanovic, *Revelation of Jesus Christ: Commentary on the Book of Revelation* (Andrews University Press), 2nd ed. [SUMMARY](./SUMMARY.md)

**Hermeneutical framework**: Historicist interpretation following Stefanovic's sanctuary-theology approach. Prophetic time uses the year-day principle (1 day = 1 year). The sanctuary in heaven structures the book's outline. Key themes: Sabbath/Creator worship vs. mark of the beast; seal of God vs. counterfeit allegiance; remnant defined by commandments of God + testimony of Jesus ([Rev. 12:17](https://biblehub.com/revelation/12-17.htm)).
